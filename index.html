Why Kahakai Watch?

Hawai‘i lacks a unified system for shoreline change. Kahakai Watch aggregates public datasets and highlights erosion risks.

Who This Helps

Students, researchers, community groups, and agencies can use this platform to make data-driven coastal decisions.

Get Involved

All datasets are open — contribute observations, drone imagery, or school projects.

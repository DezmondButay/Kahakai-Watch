Why Kahakai Watch?

Hawai'i lacks a unified system for shoreline change. Kahakai Watch aggregates public datasets and highlights erosion risks for planning and education.

Who This Helps

Students, researchers, community groups, and agencies can use this platform to make data-driven coastal decisions.

Get Involved

All datasets are open — contribute local observations, drone imagery, or school projects to improve statewide coverage.
